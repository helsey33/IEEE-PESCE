Welcome

IEEE is the world's largest professional association dedicated to advancing technological innovation and excellence for the benefit of humanity.

Technology

We bring you Technology, IEEE-PESCE members are the driving force behind providing technical expertise and innovation, driving global participation, and pursuing the ongoing advancement and promotion of new concepts.

Best Team

We have the best team who are excellent in their field on expertise and who can help you through your endeavours.

Practical Training

One thing in particular you can always expect from IEEE-PESCE is practical training, may it be working with circuits or building your own bot or perhaps hosting your personal site , we are always there for you!

What started as an elite group 10 years ago today has sprawled through out the campus of PESCE making the students technically more competitive, more professional and enhancing their abilities as an engineer. Since then IEEE-PESCE STUDENT BRANCH, a division under region 10 of IEEE has been known for the immensity with which its members were bestowed. The very success story of its students as professionals in their respective fields after graduation speaks of its standards.

EVENTS

Internet of Things

IEEE PESCE is back again with more fascinating and captivating events for the members! "If you think that the internet has changed your life, think again because the IOT is about to change it all over again!"

Treasure Hunt

We are organizing a treasure hunt for the freshers and here is your opportunity to be a part of the organising team of the treasure hunt and to conduct the most interesting treasure hunt anybody has seen so far! For more details contact:7353916507

AURIFERA

Aurifera is an IEEE PESCE magazine where student members of our branch submit articles related to science, technology, education or fiction. The articles are collected from students and the best ones are put up in AURIFERA. Read it here.Click here to read our monthly magazines.

IEEE Orientation Day

Every year IEEE PESCE conducts Orientation Day to make freshers and non IEEE students aware of the wonderful activities that are conducted by the Student Branch and pursue them to join the team and give their share of knowledge and contribution to technology and humanity

IEEE DAY

The alliance that has been formed due to working consistently towards our uplifment and gain in technical knowledge is a precious one and IEEE day is organised to celebrate this togetherness.

DEBATHON

Debatathon is an intercollegiate debate competition where individuals put forward their views and opinions on a specified topic. The event's objective is to bring out a speaker from within every member and appreciate their uniqueness.

VIKASANA

Vikasana initiative involves teaching underprivileged orphans and children at VIKASANA. Every week volunteers from IEEE PESCE go and meet the curious children of VIKASANA and help them with their studies and extracurricular activities.

WOMENS DAY

Women's Day is an annual event conducted by WIE of IEEE PESCE student branch to appreciate women and their brilliant achievements in their respective fields.It is conducted to encourage them and give examples of women showing exemplory perfomance in various fields.

WORLD PHOTOGRAPHY DAY

It is an online contest where people from different cities participate and showcase their photography skills. IEEE PESCE conducts this online activity on 19th August i.e. World Photography Day, to garner the needs of a photographer and to appreciate their immense talent.

CRESCO

CRESCO 17.0 was conducted in order to celebrate the spirit among fellow IEEE student as well as staff members. It was conducted in order to bring people together and motivate them to work togther towards technology and humanity and the betterment of society.
